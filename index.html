About Me

Designing with purpose,
shipping with impact.

I'm Haitham Fakhry, a senior graphic designer working in the field since 2008. Over this journey I've built broad experience with brands across six markets — retail, hospitality, real estate, healthcare, and technology.

My expertise centers on designing visual identities — translating ideas into complete visual experiences that reflect brand identity and serve commercial goals. My practice extends beyond design alone to cover posters, advertising banners, motion graphics, video editing, photography, UI design, and e-commerce development.

I believe successful design isn't just a pretty shape — it's an impactful tool that delivers real results. I've embraced AI tools and integrated them into my workflow to produce higher-quality work, faster, and with more innovation.
